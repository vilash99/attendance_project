FAQ for Changing Password


1. How to change your password?

1. Wait until attendance starts: Your teacher needs to activate attendance before you can change your password.

2. Find your name: Once attendance is open, look for your name in the Student name dropdown menu.

3. Click Change Password: You'll see a Change Password below Present button. Click it!

4. Enter your old and new passwords: A new window will appear. Type your current password in the Old password box, then your new password twice in the New password boxes.

5. Click "Change Password": Click the button to save your new password.

6. Take note of your new password: A box will show your new password. Take a screenshot or write it down somewhere safe!





2. What is my default password?

The default password is abc123. However, you can't use it to mark attendance. Follow the above steps to change it to a new password you choose.



3. I forgot my password.

Did you save it? Check if you took a screenshot or wrote down your new password when you changed it last time.

Still stuck? Contact the CS Department for help.



4. How to automatically entered password?

To avoid typing your password every time, you can save it in your browser. When you enter your new password and click Preset button, your browser will ask to save it. Click Save, and next time you open the attendance page, your password will be filled in automatically!
